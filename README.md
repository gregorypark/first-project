# Analyzing the Relationship Between Dominant Major and Median Earnings Within U.S. Universities

Examining the relationship between a university's dominant academic major and median graduate earnings using U.S. Department of Education College Scorecard data.

## Data Source

Data comes from the [U.S. Department of Education College Scorecard](https://collegescorecard.ed.gov/data/) (April 2023 update). 

## Repository Structure

- `notebooks/` - Jupyter notebook with complete analysis
- `figures/` - All visualizations used in paper
- `paper/` - LaTeX source and PDF
- `data/` - Instructions for downloading data (file too large to include)

## Methodology

1. **Data Cleaning**: Filtered to 4-year bachelor's-granting institutions with complete earnings data
2. **Dominant Major Classification**: Identified the academic field representing the largest share of degrees at each institution
3. **Analysis**: Computed median earnings by dominant major category and visualized distributions

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: pandas, numpy, plotnine, matplotlib
- **LaTeX**
